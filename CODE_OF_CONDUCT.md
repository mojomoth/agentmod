# Code of Conduct

agentmod is a small open-source project. We want contributing to it to be a
good experience for everyone, regardless of who you are or where you come
from.

## Expectations

- Be respectful and professional in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- Assume good faith; ask before escalating.
- Keep discussions on-topic and constructive.
- Respect maintainers' time: small, well-tested, clearly described changes.

## Unacceptable behavior

Personal attacks, demeaning or discriminatory remarks, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, spam, and sustained disruption of discussions are not
tolerated anywhere in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions and comments that
violate this code, and may temporarily or permanently ban repeat or
egregious offenders from project spaces.

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers (see SECURITY.md for the
private contact channel). Reports are handled confidentially.

## Scope

This code applies to all project spaces — the repository, issue tracker,
pull requests, and any official discussion channels — and to anyone
participating in them: maintainers, contributors, and users alike.
